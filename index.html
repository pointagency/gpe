I feel left out! I’ve never been to this month’s GJE destination, so I’m disadvantaged.

Don’t sweat it. Just enjoy other people’s creativity this month, and who knows, next month could be yours! Just make sure to follow us on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

What if I submit an image and it isn’t chosen as a finalist within a few days? Does it mean I won’t win?

No. Your image could be chosen as a finalist on any day that month. We’ll be staggering finalists and there is little correlation between when you submit, and when (or if) it gets published.

What’s to stop people from entering images they’ve pinched from Google and modified?

Images that you use or modify but don’t own the rights to are not eligible. We will verify that the Winners’ photo is original in source through Reverse Image Searches and other methods.

Who can enter and how is this competition related to the Greatest Job Ever competition?

Any Australian resident over the age of 16 is eligible. To win, not only must your submission be your own work, but you must also have entered the current month’s Greatest Job Ever competition.
